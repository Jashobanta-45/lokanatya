About Lokanatya

Your ultimate destination for seamless, stress-free ticket booking for regional performing arts

Our Mission

At Lokanatya, we believe that regional performing arts are the soul of our cultural heritage. Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional theater and modern audiences by providing a seamless, digital platform for discovering and booking tickets to spectacular performances.

We are committed to preserving and promoting the rich tradition of Jatra and other regional theatrical forms, making them accessible to audiences across generations through innovative technology and user-friendly experiences.
